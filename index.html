Research Interests

The long-term goal of my research is to apply mathematical modeling and design efficient methodologies to address problems encountered in the fields of science and engineering. Specifically, I am interested in the following research topics:

  • Mathematical Modeling and Simulation in Materials Science: Focusing on materials science and biomedical science.

  • Scientific Machine Learning: Machine learning for solving PDEs, Operator learning, Understanding neural networks through phenomenon-driven theoretical research.

  • AI for Science: Generative models for sampling, Generative models with applications in data-driven modeling.
